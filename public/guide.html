Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers regarding local tour permits, refunds, and transportation options:

Do I need special permits for mountain trails? +
Yes, certain high-altitude peak passes at Solitude Ridge require environmental entry passes. These can be pre-obtained during our check-out booking process.
What is the local refund and cancellation policy? +
We offer full refunds for trip cancellations processed at least 72 hours prior to scheduled departure dates. Accommodation refunds are dependent on individual hotel policies.
Are local transport shuttle facilities provided? +
Yes, all premium and luxury bookings include complimentary airport shuttles and localized daily transfers between your hotel and the attractions.
Is there 24/7 client booking support available? +
Absolutely. Our cloud-deployed booking backend system guarantees uptime and is constantly supported by our support staff via live chat.
