Frequently Inquired

Answers to administrative queries regarding entry permits, refunds, and transportation:

Are entry permits required for alpine valleys? +
Yes, certain preservation zones around Elysian Peaks require state environmental permits, which are bundled and generated during our booking checkouts.
What is the standard cancellation window? +
We offer full cancellation refunds on directory tickets processed 72 hours before departure. Hotel room refunds vary by accommodation provider.
Do bookings include regional transit shuttle? +
All luxury suite and standard lodge reservations include standard airport transfers and daily connection shuttles between lodging and coordinates.
